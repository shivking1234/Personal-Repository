Projects

Object Detection

My object detection program utilizes advanced deep learning models and image processing techniques to accurately identify and locate objects in images and video streams. With a user-friendly interface, users can upload images or access live video feeds for real-time analysis. The program can detect a wide range of objects, and users have the flexibility to customize detection parameters. Privacy and security are prioritized as all processing is performed locally. The program also provides detailed reports with annotated images and object statistics. Through this project, I aim to contribute to the advancement of computer vision and its applications in diverse fields.

AI Tic Tac Toe

My AI Tic-Tac-Toe is an innovative twist on the classic game, incorporating artificial intelligence to provide a challenging and engaging player experience. Using a sophisticated algorithm, the AI opponent adapts its strategies based on the player's moves, ensuring an exciting and dynamic gameplay. The user interface is intuitive and interactive, allowing players to make their moves effortlessly. The AI's decision-making process is lightning-fast, providing real-time responses and maintaining a seamless gaming experience.
